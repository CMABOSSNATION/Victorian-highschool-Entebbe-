Our Story

A School Born from Vision & Partnership

Victorian High School Entebbe was founded in February 2008 through a unique Ugandan–German partnership. It stands as a beacon of inclusive, holistic education in Entebbe — offering not just academic excellence, but a full human experience rooted in its powerful motto: "More Than Education."

The school is supported by the Lernplus Foundation, a registered NGO overseeing development from both Uganda and Germany — giving students a genuinely international perspective right here in Entebbe.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Core Values

🎯

Our Mission

To avail education services based on sustainable, viable and competitive terms to students of all walks of life — making quality secondary education accessible to everyone.

🌟

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution in Uganda that goes beyond academics — producing globally aware, morally upright, and professionally capable graduates.

🤝

Our Approach

Through our Ugandan–German partnership and Lernplus Foundation support, we bring an international perspective to Entebbe — giving students a world-class foundation for life.
